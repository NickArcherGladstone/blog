About

The Daily Brief is an independent publication by Nicholas Archer covering politics, the stock market, current events, and sports.

The goal is simple: provide honest, well-reasoned analysis you can trust. No partisan spin, no clickbait, no agenda beyond getting it right.

What I Cover

  • Politics — Elections, legislation, policy analysis, and geopolitics. The focus is on what matters and why, not on the daily noise.
  • Markets — Stock market outlook, sector analysis, and what economic signals mean for your portfolio.
  • News — The stories that deserve your attention, with context that helps you understand them.
  • Sports — Football and golf analysis, fantasy football strategy, and in-season coverage.

Why This Exists

There's no shortage of news. There is a shortage of sources that earn your trust through consistency, transparency, and a commitment to getting it right over getting clicks.

That's what I'm building here.
